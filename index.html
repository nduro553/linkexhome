Complete Drywall Installation

We provide full-scale drywall installation services for new construction projects, additions, and renovations. Our technicians measure precisely, cut accurately, and install efficiently to create smooth, even surfaces ready for finishing. We work with various drywall types including standard, moisture-resistant, and fire-rated panels to meet your specific needs.

Expert Repair Solutions

From small holes and cracks to water damage and structural issues, our repair specialists can restore your walls to their original condition. We match textures seamlessly and ensure repairs blend invisibly with existing surfaces. No job is too small or too complex for our experienced team.

Texture Matching Services

One of the most challenging aspects of drywall repair is matching existing textures. Our craftsmen have mastered various texture techniques including orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, and smooth finishes. We take pride in creating repairs that are virtually undetectable.

Taping and Mudding

Proper taping and mudding are essential for achieving professional results. We apply multiple coats with precision, sanding between each layer to create perfectly smooth joints and corners. Our attention to detail ensures a flawless foundation for painting or wallpapering.

Ceiling Repairs

Ceiling damage can be particularly challenging to repair. Whether dealing with water stains, cracks, or sagging sections, our team has the specialized equipment and expertise to restore ceilings safely and effectively. We handle popcorn ceiling removal and retexturing as well.

Commercial Projects

We serve commercial clients with efficient, professional drywall services that minimize disruption to business operations. Our team can work flexible hours to accommodate your schedule and complete projects within tight deadlines while maintaining quality standards.

Our Journey Through The Years

The story of LinkexHome is one of steady growth, unwavering commitment to quality, and deep roots in the community we serve. What began as a small operation has evolved into a comprehensive drywall service provider, but our core values remain unchanged.

2008

Marcus Hendrickson established LinkexHome with a single truck and a commitment to excellence. The first year focused on building relationships with local contractors and homeowners, completing 47 projects and establishing a reputation for reliability.

2011

Expanded operations to include commercial projects after successfully completing a major office renovation downtown. Hired three additional technicians and invested in specialized equipment for larger-scale installations. This year marked our transition from a solo operation to a growing team.

2014

Reached a milestone of 1,000 completed projects. Introduced advanced texture matching techniques and began offering specialized services for historic building restoration. Received recognition from the local builders association for outstanding craftsmanship.

2017

Opened a dedicated training program for apprentices, ensuring the next generation of drywall professionals maintains the high standards we've established. Expanded service area to cover three additional counties based on customer demand.

2020

Despite challenging times, adapted operations to continue serving customers safely. Implemented contactless estimates and flexible scheduling. Completed over 400 projects that year, helping homeowners improve their living spaces.

2023

Celebrated 15 years in business with a team of 12 skilled technicians. Introduced eco-friendly drywall options and sustainable practices. Continued commitment to quality has resulted in over 85% of business coming from referrals and repeat customers.

Our Detailed Work Process

At LinkexHome, we believe transparency and communication are essential to successful projects. Our systematic approach ensures consistent results and keeps you informed every step of the way. From initial consultation to final walkthrough, we maintain the highest standards of professionalism.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your project. During this phase, we listen carefully to your needs, examine the work area, take measurements, and discuss your timeline and expectations. This consultation is always free and comes with no obligation.

Step 2: Detailed Estimate

Based on our assessment, we provide a comprehensive written estimate that breaks down materials, labor, and timeline. We explain every aspect of the proposal and answer all questions. Our estimates are detailed and transparent with no hidden fees.

Step 3: Project Preparation

Once you approve the estimate, we schedule the work at your convenience. We protect your property with drop cloths and plastic sheeting, move furniture as needed, and ensure the work area is properly prepared. Our team arrives on time with all necessary materials and equipment.

Step 4: Expert Installation

Our skilled technicians execute the work with precision and care. We follow industry best practices for hanging, taping, mudding, and finishing. Throughout the process, we maintain a clean work environment and minimize disruption to your daily routine.

Step 5: Quality Inspection

Before considering any job complete, we conduct a thorough quality inspection. We check for smooth surfaces, proper texture matching, and clean joints. Any imperfections are addressed immediately to ensure the finished product meets our exacting standards.

Step 6: Final Walkthrough

We walk through the completed project with you, ensuring you're completely satisfied with the results. We clean up thoroughly, removing all debris and leaving your space ready for the next phase. Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. Click here to continue reading what our customers say.
